About ESLSCA University

Preparing world-class calibers since 1949 in France

Founded in 1949, ESLSCA (Ecole Supérieure Libre des Sciences Commerciales Appliquées) has been known for providing a comprehensive academic experience to its students, favoring abilities over background. Over the years, we have and continue to adopt students' evolving needs.

Our mission is to prepare students for a meaningful and interconnected professional and personal life balance, by equipping them with skills, experience and the confidence to achieve their fullest potential.

Word from the President

ESLSCA Business School since its inception in 1949 has emerged as a major player within the educational system in France. Starting from the 1960's ESLSCA started to internationalize their programs and to develop global partners in education.

We at ESLSCA University realize the significant challenges that we are facing in education and are well prepared to meet them and turn them into innovative opportunities for all stakeholders: faculty, students, researchers, business community, among others.

Last but not least always remember that you are our lifelong ambassadors, ESLSCIANS today and forever.

Nadia Elaref, Ph. D.

President, ESLSCA University
